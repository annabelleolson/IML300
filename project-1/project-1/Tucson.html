Balancing the duties he feels to the miraculous woman that raised him, and the duty he feels to his daughter to
condemn her backwards beliefs and incautious actions. And I strive to just be there. I don't define myself at this
moment as a daughter or granddaughter, but as someone who admires my father and is there to support.
