Tucker County, West Virgina

In West Virginia, I climb. I see my father nearing the summit and I follow. I climb above rolling smoking hills and look down on the town I had slept in the night before. From this new perspective, I see things I had neglected to notice before. And suddenly I bumped into my father who had stopped his summit ascent to point out to me details below.
